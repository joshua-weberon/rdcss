I came to the United States 35 years ago, as an engineering student. As I left India and my loving middle-class family, I carried with me the value of dreaming BIG and persevering that dream with determination and deep dedication. I am at a very comfortable place today. However, the one thing that still haunts me is the awareness that 45% of the children around the world die of malnutrition (source: worldhunger.org). Even though we have enough food supply to feed 7.7 billion people, we let these children die every hour. Is it not deeply disconcerting to say the least?


The idea of making a difference started with my visit to our housemaid's hut, devoid of all the basic necessities, in addition to the lack of a bathroom. The faces of her two malnourished children held the blueprint to their everyday life compelling me to think about sharing. It seems magical that these kids are now grown up healthy, educated, and have settled comfortably, carrying forward what they received in generosity.


Encouraged by this real-life experience, we are personally committed to the mission of 'lifting' these most deserving children. Your referrals will be the most valuable endeavor. Our professional commitment to you is that we earnestly provide outstanding results to our buyers & sellers.
